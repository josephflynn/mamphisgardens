Memphis Gardens is a creative studio in Abbotsford.

The studio cultivates creative individuals and small businesses in Melbourne ranging from design, fashion, art, and architecture to web and app development and more.

Location:

37 Yarra Street, Abbotsford, VIC, 3067

Situated around the corner from Collingwood Train Station, Gahan Reserve and close to near by cafes, restaurants, and art supply stores. It is a stone's throw away from Victoria and Johnston Streets, and the Abbotsford Convent. With Smith and Brunswick Street precincts in Fitzroy only a short walk away.

What comes with a studio:

Memphis Gardens Provides an abundance of communally useable spaces and work areas. These include a tools workshop area for building things, a wet studio for ceramics and clay work, a photography studio for shooting products, models or artworks, a kitchen area with communal table and chairs, a sound proof room for private meetings and phone calls, free WiFi access to the Internet, as well as other basic amenities.

We also have an in house gallery space Memphis Projects, which can be used by studio tenants for exhibitions, product launches and events. The central space is available for use of events, workshops, performances and other creative endeavours.

Design:

The Studio's layout and interior was designed with the intention of creating as much of a naturally illuminated, modular environment for creative practitioners from all fields as possible.

Currently housing 10 individual large studios including a mezzanine level. Desk spaces will become available soon along with another addition of 6 smaller studios to be housed in a 'green house' style central studio hub.

Please Contact Us if you are interested in renting a studio space, or for more information.
