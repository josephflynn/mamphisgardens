Proposals





Memphis Projects is the gallery space in Memphis Gardens Studios.

The gallery showcases contemporary artists work from a broad range of creative practices including painting, drawing, printmaking, installation, sculpture, photography and more.

We are currently taking proposals for new and exciting exhibition ideas that push the boundaries of contemporary art.

Please download our Proposals Information Package for more information on how to submit an application to exhibit as well as on the pricing and size of the space.

* Download the Exhibition Proposal Guide *

What to include in your proposal:

* A summary of your proposed project in 250 words or less
* 250 word max description of the form in which the work will take (what will be seen, heard, experienced etc)
* A CV for each participating artist, including contact details
* A maximum of 20 jpeg images, and web links of up to five minutes of video.
* Image list of works with material deatils

Please email proposals to memphisgardens.projects@gmail.com
