Special Edition

Mclean Stephenson & Alexander Gow

22nd February - 23rd February

Download the Catalogue


Mclean Stephenson is a Sydney based photographer.
With inspirations varying from the surrealists to contemporaries like Wolfgang Tillmans; Stephenson's photographic work explores the inner landscapes of the mind and challenges the traditions of photography.
In 2015 he published a book of photographic works titled Extracts, Vol. 1
Instagram: @McleanStephenson

Alexander Gow is a respected ARIA award winning songwriter.
Gow's first explorations into photography are displayed in this exhibition as a testament to his creativity, not only as a musician.
Gow is a Melbourne based artist.
Instagram @AlexanderGow
