Construction Lines II - Shifting Perspective

Jo Harrison

8th June - 24th June

Download the Catalogue

Download the Artist's CV


Developing over a number of years and forming the next part in a series of solo exhibitions, by Jo Harrison, this site-specific installation takes the form of a three dimensional 'drawing' imposed onto the conventional gallery space.

The work explores the linear and spatial depth of the gallery through the use of industrial materials as a progression in this series of structural responses.

Jo Harrison has exhibitied in over 28 group and solo exhibitions throughout the UK and Australia since 1997. She has continued her Arts Education since 1992, along the way gaining a Bachelor of Arts with Honours in Painting from the Wimbeldon School of Art and Bath College of Art in 1995 and a Master of Art from the Manchester Metropolitan University in 2007.

Harrison's work spans from painting to drawing and installation to sound. This recent exhibition she is presenting at Memphis Projects, will be the second installment of "Construction Lines" following on from her solo show in 2016 at Dark Horse Experiment.
